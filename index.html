What is Academic Decathlon?

Academic Decathlon is a 10 event competition consisting of 10 different events: 7 multiple choice tests, an essay, a speech, and an interview.

The multiple choice tests are based on resource guides (essentially mini textbooks) based on a given theme like Climate Change or the Roaring Twenties.
